-lite version-
(aka; easy to compile version for windows)

For windows:
step 1 - open VC project in build directory. *note* for gcc you must change config.h from MSC to GCC.
step 2 - compile desired configurations.

For *NOT* windows:
-you must manually configure config.h and/or noconfig.h, or 
compile from the full mcpp 2.7.2 sources using the provided makefiles.
